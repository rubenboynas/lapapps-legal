## 1. Information Collection and Use

The Application collects information when you download and use it. This information may include:

* Your device's Internet Protocol address (e.g. IP address)
* The pages of the Application that you visit, the time and date of your visit, the time spent on those pages
* The operating system you use on your mobile device

### 1.1. Microphone and Audio Data
The Application requires access to your device's **Microphone** to perform its core functionality (measuring decibel levels and analyzing audio frequencies).
* **Local Processing:** Audio data is processed in real-time locally on your device.
* **No Storage:** The Application does **not** record, store, or transmit raw audio data to the Service Provider's servers or any third party. The microphone is used strictly for measurement purposes.

### 1.2. Bluetooth Data
The Application requires access to **Bluetooth** permissions to connect with external audio devices if necessary for the app functionality.

### 1.3. Location Data
The Application **does not** gather precise information about the location of your mobile device (GPS).

The Service Provider may use the information you provided to contact you from time to time to provide you with important information, required notices, and marketing promotions.
